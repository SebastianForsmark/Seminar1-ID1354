Grandma's Swedish Meatballs a lá Grandpa

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams of ground beef
  • 1 whole white onion
  • 2 cups mashed potatoes
  • 2 dl milk
  • 2 tablespoons of salt
  • 2 eggs
  • Some brown sauce of unknown origin

Instructions:

  1. Heat oven to 280°C. Line 30x20cm pan with foil; spray with cooking spray.
  2. In large bowl, mix all ingredients. Shape mixture into 20 to 24 (eyeball sized) meatballs. Place 2 cm apart in pan.
  3. Bake uncovered 18 to 22 minutes or until no longer pink in center.
  4. Throw meatballs violently into the mashed potatoes
  5. Pour copious amounts of brown sauce over your creation and think about what you've done.
